About Calm Companion

What is Calm Companion?

Calm Companion is an AI-powered support tool designed to help you manage everyday stress and anxiety. We believe everyone deserves access to gentle, judgment-free support whenever they need it.

Our mission is to provide a safe, welcoming space where you can express your feelings, learn coping strategies, and reconnect with your inner calm‚Äîall at your own pace.

How It Works

Calm Companion combines conversation, guided exercises, and evidence-based techniques to help you:

  • Talk through your worries in a confidential, non-judgmental space
  • Learn breathing and grounding techniques to calm your nervous system
  • Reframe negative thoughts with more compassionate perspectives
  • Develop a personalized toolkit of strategies that work for you
